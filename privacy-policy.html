Privacy Policy

PlankyFit: Daily Plank Timer
Last updated: April 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the PlankyFit: Daily Plank Timer mobile application (“App”) handles information when you use the App. We keep collection minimal.

1. Who we are

The App is operated by the developer of PlankyFit: Daily Plank Timer. For privacy questions, contact us at the email address listed in the App Store listing or your support channel.

2. Information we do not collect on our own servers

We do not operate a login system in the App and we do not maintain our own user database for analytics or advertising. Optional profile details you enter (such as display name or a profile photo) and your workout preferences are stored locally on your device unless otherwise noted below.

3. RevenueCat (subscriptions)

We use RevenueCat, a third-party service, to validate in-app purchases and subscription status. RevenueCat may process:

  • An anonymous app user identifier (for example, a RevenueCat customer identifier) that is not your name or email;
  • Purchase-related information provided by Apple (such as whether you have an active subscription and product identifiers);
  • Approximate location derived from IP address (for example, country or region) for fraud prevention, tax, and pricing compliance.

RevenueCat’s handling of data is described in their privacy policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy.

4. Apple & payments

Subscriptions are billed and managed by Apple through your Apple ID. We do not receive or store your full payment card details. Apple’s privacy policy applies to payment and account data Apple processes.

5. Push notifications

If you enable plank reminders, the App uses Apple’s notification services to schedule local reminders on your device. Granting notification permission is optional. We do not use push notifications for third-party advertising.

6. Children’s privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, contact us so we can take appropriate steps.

7. Your choices

  • Disable notifications in iOS Settings or inside the App.
  • Manage or cancel subscriptions in Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions on your device.
  • Delete the App to remove locally stored App data from your device (subscription records managed by Apple/RevenueCat may remain subject to their retention policies).

8. International users

RevenueCat and Apple may process data in the United States and other countries where they operate. By using the App, you acknowledge that such processing may occur.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will change when we do. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated policy.

10. Contact
